- **Multi-chain Presence**: Ethereum, Solana, Cosmos, and beyond
- **Institutional Adoption**: Major financial institutions using maxBTC for BTC yield exposure
- **TradFi Strategy Integration**: Bringing traditional finance yield strategies to BTC holders
- **Self-Sustaining Ecosystem**: Complete with governance, insurance, and community-driven development
