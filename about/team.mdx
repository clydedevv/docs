## Our Background

Structured was built by core contributors to [Lido](https://lido.fi) and [P2P.ORG](https://p2p.org), organizations that have pioneered secure and scalable solutions in the liquid staking space.

The team combines:
- Deep technical expertise in smart contract development
- Experience managing billions in crypto assets
- Strong understanding of trading strategies and risk management
- Track record of building successful DeFi products

## Mission

Our mission is to tokenize high-yield strategies, making them accessible, liquid, and composable for a wider range of users. We're starting with Bitcoin, but our vision extends to a diverse range of strategies and assets.
