**How It Works**

The maxBTC minting contract forms the core of our horizontal scaling approach, creating powerful network effects through a unified architecture:

- **Single Point of Entry**: All maxBTC tokens originate from one central minting contract, managing multiple underlying vaults through a standardized interface
- **Unified DEX Liquidity**: Concentrates liquidity in a single asset rather than fragmenting across multiple tokens, creating deeper markets
- **Optimized Capital Allocation**: Automatically distributes capital across strategies based on yield and risk parameters
- **DeFi Integrations**: Simplifies integration across lending platforms, yield aggregators, and other DeFi protocols
- **Modular Vault Architecture**: Each yield strategy operates as an independent vault that can exist on any chain while contributing to the same maxBTC token

## Market Opportunity

Perpetual markets have found product-market fit in crypto and are here to stay. These markets fundamentally need liquidity and reward liquidity providers with higher rates than lower-risk options like staking and lending.

However, these liquidity pools lack clear directional exposure to desired assets (e.g., Jupiter JLP pool represents a mix of BTC, ETH, SOL, and USDC/T) and require active management.

maxBTC brings superior yields generated by perpetual markets' liquidity pools to users who prefer clear directional exposure to Bitcoin.

**Strategy Expansion Process**

As maxBTC grows, new strategies are integrated through a defined governance process:

1. **Strategy Proposal**: New yield strategies are proposed and technically vetted
2. **Risk Assessment**: Evaluation of risk parameters, including correlation with existing strategies
3. **Capacity Allocation**: Each approved strategy receives an allocation cap
4. **Gradual Integration**: New strategies are introduced with conservative caps that increase with proven performance
